题目: Fractional Calculus: From Classical Theory to Forefront
摘要: In this talk, we first give a brief review of the early development of the Fractional Calculus. Then, based on the well-known results of Saigo and Kiryakova, we show the core idea of the Generalized Fractional Calculus and illustrate how it has evolved over the last few decades to become an important part of the modern theory of Fractional Calculus. We also report our latest work on a certain class of fractional integral operators whose kernels involve a very special class of generalized hypergeometric functions. Finally, we point out some problems for further research.
